VIDEOJET

Videojet Technologies is a world-leading manufacturer of coding and marking solutions. With more than 345,000 units installed worldwide, Videojet covers all major technologies, including continuous and thermal ink jet, laser, thermal transfer overprinting and case coding.

INKJET CODING CONSUMABLES

Videojet products and services allow its customers to apply variable data such as best-by date, production date, lot number and operator information, as well as linear and 2D barcodes, to virtually any kind of product packaging, shipping container or pallet. Since joining the Danaher portfolio in 2002, Videojet has developed the new gold standard in track and trace coding solutions, garnered a Best Technological Innovation award at the Interphex Exhibitor Awards and rolled out innovative developments like Long Life Core technology. With more than 345,000 units installed worldwide, Videojet has over 4,000 employees in 30 direct operations around the world.
