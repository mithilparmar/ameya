Packaging of Food, Cosmetics and Medical items have the highest requirements of health, safety and environment. Our Packaging segment is focused on providing products which follow the highest levels of safety and compliance to satisfy consumer safety. The packaging involves wide ranges of products from mono carton packages (paper) to secondary packages to printing on metal which are exposed to a wide range of conditions including freezing. Ameya helps manufacturers choose the best inks, coatings, varnishes and adhesives in the segment which protect the consumers the most . It has been a wonderful journey with the converting industry by helping them move to better products with focus on Health, Safety, and Environment and quality along with local service which is a must in this segment for customer delight. Packaging is going to be a fast growing sector in India for many years and the two aspects of marketing and safety will go hand in hand to define the products features and with Siegwerk Inks we are confident of going the extra mile to fulfil these core requirements.
