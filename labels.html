LABELS


The Label segment and our presence here describes Ameya’s principles perfectly. We bring the best inks which ensure the highest standard of quality and adherence to safety for health and the environment.


INTERESTED IN SEEING HOW AMEYA CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE FOR YOU?




Regardless of whether the product is a shower gel, a beverage bottle or skin cream in a tube the printed label or the tube laminate is the face of the product and plays a significant part in its sale. The wide range for the narrow web sector is supplemented by special products for achieving tactile and visual effects. Ameya offers one of the best Inks in the world with complete range be it Water Based or UV or the latest LED Inks for Labels along with all ancillary products be it UV Cleaners, Coatings, or Benzophenone free Varnishes. The focus always remains on Environment and Human Health by offering products with the highest safety standards in the world with one of the best compliance track records. Labels are also a fast growing market which have increased focus on marketing and safety, which is our products forte.
