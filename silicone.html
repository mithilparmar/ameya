Silicones is a business segment that Ameya forayed into since its inception. The major products where Ameya is active are DMPS (Silicone Oil), Silicone Defoamers Silicone Release Agents, Heat Stable Silicone Oils, Paintable Release Oils Emulsions, Electrical Resins, Thread Lubricants, and Sugar Defoamers too. Defoamers are widely used in water treatment infrastructure which helps in the reduction of pump cavitations prevention of loss due to overflow, and improvement of sanitation and safety. The silicone oil market is anticipated to witness growth driven by many factors like rise in demand in cosmetics, personal care and rapid increase in application areas. Ameya’s depth in Silicones is unparalleled amongst any Marketing Company.
