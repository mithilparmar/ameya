MARKETS

Ameya Perfomatt’s “11 core markets” are the Business Units where we have established a stronghold. We are a unique company which is focused on specialty materials and when a product goes through the commoditization cycle; we get out of it. We have introduced upwards of 20 new technologies in India. Our uniqueness also lies in the mixture of relationship strength and exploring new sectors where we can play a role. The Company’s core strategy is to deepen our relationship with existing customers and constantly find new areas where Ameya can create value in Indian markets.


HYGIENE

The Hygiene sector consists of the Baby Diaper, Female Hygiene and Adult Incontinence Product market. This is a fast growing market in India due to the rise in incomes and literacy rates. Ameya is targeting this segment with a variety of products and offerings under a single roof.

PSA

The PSA market includes Tapes, Medical Tapes, Holographic Labels, Jumbo Bags, Labels, Grain Bags etc. Heat insulation, appropriate sealing, resistance against moisture, excellent strength is some of the properties associated with pressure sensitive adhesives. Ameya has been able to bring almost all PSA technologies under a single roof.

LABELS

The printed label or the tube laminate is the face of the product and plays a significant part in its sale. Ameya offers one of the best Inks in the world with complete range be it Water Based or UV or the latest LED Inks for Labels along with all ancillary products be it UV Cleaners, Coatings, or Benzophenone free Varnishes.

CORRUGATION

India’s corrugated box industry is an inevitable part of the manufacturing sector which relies heavily on corrugated packaging for finished goods transportation and handling. When customers invest into state of the art machines and want to have the best inks to efficiently run those machines at extremely high speeds to print sharp images that’s when they get delighted by Ameya’s offerings with Siegwerk as its partner along with extremely good service by localized blending of Inks.

PRINTING

India is among the fast growing printing ink markets globally spurred by the rapid expansion of the domestic print markets. Ameya has been in the Printing segment since Ameya’s inception By partnering with Siegwerk we are able to provide highest quality and service which helps our customers produce excellent quality on a consistent basis.

PACKAGING

Packaging of Food, Cosmetics and Medical items have the highest requirements of health, safety and environment. Our Packaging segment is focused on providing products which follow the highest levels of safety and compliance to satisfy consumer safety. Ameya helps manufacturers choose the best inks, coatings, varnishes and adhesives in the segment which protect the consumers the most.

ICE CREAM SLEEVES

The healthy growth of the market can be attributed to numerous forces: Population growth, rising disposable incomes, urbanisation rates, changing consumer preferences, improved cold supply chain, etc are some of the factors that are broadening the growth aspects of the market. Ameya offers adhesives which protect the consumer from harm. Ameya offers one of the best products in the industry which can withstand extreme low temperatures and yet is the most effective in the market.

SILICONE

Silicones is a business segment that Ameya forayed into since its inception. The major products where Ameya is active are DMPS (Silicone Oil), Silicone Defoamers Silicone Release Agents, Heat Stable Silicone Oils, Paintable Release Oils Emulsions, Electrical Resins, Thread Lubricants, and Sugar Defoamers too. The silicone oil market is anticipated to witness growth driven by many factors like rise in demand in cosmetics, personal care and rapid increase in application areas.

END OF LINE

End of line packaging assembly is used in the manufacturing to automating the final leg of packaging prior to the final transportation of goods.Ameya partners with the leading company of the world now for more than 2 decades to bring state-of-the-art products to Indian Manufacturing Sector.

ALUMINIUM FINISHING

Ameya offers a wide range of products for process plant cleaning The portfolio of highly efficient cleaners and pickling agents is suitable for use on tanks/baths and equipment made from steel, stainless steel and aluminium. Regular plant cleaning with our speciality chemicals ensures high process efficiency and extends the service life of the pre treatment line.

HEALTHCARE

Health Care market in India is a fast growing sector due to rising incomes, better health awareness, lifestyle diseases and increasing access to insurance. Ameya is into NonWoven medical fabric for gowns, coveralls, and wound care markets.
