OUR HYGIENE MARKET SEGMENTS

BABY DIAPER

FEMALE SANITARY PADS

ADULT INCONTINENCE PRODUCTS


The Hygiene sector consists of the Baby Diaper, Female Hygiene and Adult Incontinence Product market. This is a fast growing market in India due to the rise in incomes and literacy rates. Ameya is targeting this segment with a variety of products and offerings under a single roof. Excellent relationships and service are key for establishing a stronghold which has been our achievement in a short span in Hygiene markets. The primary products here are Non Woven, Films, Adhesives, Fluff pulp, SAP and elastic. The manufacturers in the Hygiene sector have increased awareness on quality and are more receptive to change which lead to an increase in hygiene of the product. Ameya in a short span has been able to gain a hold over this market and is looking to be an indispensable pitstop for all the raw material required for this sector. Ameya plans to introduce new technologies to Indian mainstream like HAP and Sap sheet and take our economical quality products to the world as well to provide an option for a source of diversification.
