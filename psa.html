The PSA market includes Labels, Tapes, Medical Tapes, Holographic Labels, Jumbo Bags, Grain Bags etc. Heat insulation, appropriate sealing, resistance against moisture, excellent strength is some of the properties associated with pressure sensitive adhesives. Tapes is the most popular application segment in the pressure sensitive adhesives market. Pressure sensitive tapes offer various advantages such as reduced product weight, improved product durability, enhanced product aesthetics, lower production costs, and increased productivity. Ameya has been able to bring almost all PSA technologies under a single roof. Ameya is also exploring eco-friendly and biodegradable PSA which can play a pivotal role in the time where the need to innovate and have sustainable solutions is paramount.
