LEADERSHIP

Ameya was founded and is headed by Gautam and Bharat Bafna - two brothers. Having been brought up in a large joint business family with various businesses including large manufacturing setup, mid-size retail setups as well as marketing setups – during their upbringing itself they had seen many ups and downs of various types of businesses. Building a business seemed a natural progression for them – but should it be manufacturing, should be marketing or retail was the only question. After thorough analysis and mapping they decided to venture into Market Making while keeping innovation always as center piece to strategy & focus.



Gautam Bafna

Managing Director

Gautam Bafna is the Managing Director of Ameya. Methodical, Detail-oriented, Savvy and Affable are some of the top qualities of Mr. Gautam. Personal warmth and relationships are areas where he excels. An able administrator, he is like the engine of the Ameya boat. Having a keen knowledge of tax and administration he has an affinity for the details and the nitty gritty which is essential for a clear mind and future mindedness.


Bharat Bafna

CEO

Bharat Bafna is the CEO of Ameya. Dynamic, Sharp, Tech-Savvy and Extroverted are some of the best ways to describe Mr. Bharat. Practicality and thinking agility are areas where he excels. A problem solver, he keeps the engine well-oiled and charts the course for the Ameya boat. Having an analytical and innovative mind he is great at structure, presentation and the art of strategic thinking which is vital for our march ahead.

Gautam & Bharat are both commerce graduates having schooled, graduated and lived in a variety of places allowing them to have a much broader perspective and understanding of various cultures of the country. One of the reasons for their success is this variety of exposure, thanks to their upbringing at different places of the country. Masters of comprehension; both of them bring with them all-round abilities which complement each other thus leading Ameya in the best possible way. Both being multilingual also helps in local markets where the local language can provide a crucial advantage. The Duo is leading the Pack which is Hungry for more.
