HISTORY OF AMEYA

The history from foundation 1998 to today.


Business Segments

Ameya began operations and started Vat Dyes, Inks, Masterbatches, Coding & Inkjets (known as Willett then and Videojet now) & Silicone Businesses.


Company Growth

From a team size of 4 people in a span of 3~4 years Ameya grew to around 25 people with 1 additional business every year with successful commercialization but mainly operating in Gujarat.

Business Segments

Ameya added at least around 10 new businesses & created markets for those. In most cases the strategy was of introducing completely new brands in India, usually from small European manufacturers against a mighty MNC already present strongly in India. Thus, we formed new divisions like Capital Equipment & Construction Specialty Materials.


Company Growth

Grew From 4 Business Unit’s to around 10 Business Unit’s and a completely professional organization with Independent profit center heads (Business Unit Heads) with Pan India presence & offices spread across the country including all Metro cities.





Recognition

Won many accolades and awards from most Partners.






Company Strategy

Grew from a team size of 30 to 170 with 4 offices in Vadodara & that necessitated a move for a Corporate office under one roof, one identity & one address which could reflect our ambitions & our journey. Thus we started building an office ultimately resulting in Ameya Towers.

Business Consolidation

It was time to update our strategy from organic to inorganic growth while consolidating last decades high growth into profitable growth with focus on healthy businesses where the company wanted to build strengths & market share for the long term.





Company Growth

4 Acquisitions were the key highlights in this span of around 7 years which included a Specialty Sector Manufacturing setup, 2 distribution businesses (competitor) & 1 highly specialized business in Defence Aviation bought from MNC.


Company Strategy

The sharpening of focus on Profitability & Automation apart from decentralization & becoming highly process driven organization led to sharp reduction in team size & increase in efficiency – rendering unutilized space in Corporate Tower and also rendering a need for operating from single seamless floor which also had to reflect the culture of organization – an open & embracing culture.




End of a Journey, Beginning of Another

That’s when the need for an office which reflected this overhauled culture was born. Thus we started building Ameya House keeping the above in mind.

Company Strategy

Having consolidated our journey towards profitability, sustainable growth started again with more new additions while remaining lean & agile as an organization.





Company Growth

Addition of 1~2 product, segment every year – after indepth research not only from Market need / acceptance point but also from Healthy business, profitability & sustainability aspects point of view were done before adding any Business / Business Unit.





Watch out for the next big thing in the offing.
