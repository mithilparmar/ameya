India is among the fast growing printing ink markets globally spurred by the rapid expansion of the domestic print markets. Backed by a strong demand from key end user segments such as package printing, newsprint, publishing and other commercial printing, the printing ink market in India has registered strong growth in the recent years. Ameya has been in the Printing segment since Ameya’s inception. By partnering with Siegwerk we are able to provide highest quality and service which helps our customers produce excellent quality on a consistent basis. We serve our customers who use our products in all end uses of sheetfed offset printing applications which include printing on letterhead, business card, stationary, brochures, flyers, business forms, calendars and diaries among others.
