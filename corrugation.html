CORRUGATED TRAYS


India’s corrugated box industry is an inevitable part of the manufacturing sector which relies heavily on corrugated packaging for finished goods transportation and handling. Factors such as increasing demand from fresh food and beverages, home personal care goods, electronic goods industries, logistics application, increasing consumer awareness towards sustainable packaging and growth of the e commerce industry have propelled the growth of Indian corrugated boxes market. When customers invest into state of the art machines and want to have the best inks to efficiently run those machines at extremely high speeds to print sharp images that’s when they get delighted by Ameya’s offerings with Siegwerk as its partner along with extremely good service by localized blending of Inks.
